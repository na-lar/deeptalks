How We Respond

Flags

Flags is a format where you raise a colored flag to signal how you want to respond, and the speaker chooses who goes next. Flags can be combined creatively to express complex responses. (e.g. green + red = agree and disagree)

Red — Disagree / Counterargument

You want to challenge what was said or offer an opposing view.

Green — Agree / Elaborate

You agree and want to build on or expand the point being made.

Yellow — Question for the speaker

You have a question directed at the person who just spoke.

Blue — Not Directed at Speaker

You want to shift direction, or respond to an earlier point made. Can be combined — e.g. Blue + Yellow for a group question.
