About Nalar

Nalar — the Indonesian word for reason and rational thought — is a spin-off of Deep Talks for those drawn to logic, critical thinking, and rigorous inquiry.

We dig into arguments, dismantle assumptions, and try to think more clearly about the world. Expect debates, structured discussions, and the occasional paradigm shift.
