About Love Talks

Love Talks is a spin-off of Deep Talks dedicated to one of life's most complex subjects: love. We explore romantic love, self-love, loss, attachment, connection, and everything in between — honestly, vulnerably, and without judgement.

This is a space to speak your truth and hear others'. Whether you're navigating heartbreak, falling hard, or just trying to understand what love even means to you — you're welcome here.
